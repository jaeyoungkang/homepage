This week, I spent more time organizing my identity than working on the product. I completely redefined the page that had introduced me as a "data consultant." I removed the consulting sections and put up the name AI Product Producer.

## What I did this week

- Full repositioning of personal homepage
- Introduced Hugo-based blog structure
- Content direction: documenting the process of exploring and developing

## What I learned

Changing your positioning isn't just about editing copy. It was about redefining "what kind of person am I." I realized that positioning yourself as someone pioneering a new field, rather than a finished expert, is more honest and more sustainable.
